Our services

Experience Authentic Sardinian Fishing Tourism

Discover the best of coastal Sardinia with our comprehensive range of services. From traditional fishing expeditions to snorkeling adventures and fresh seafood dining, every experience is designed to connect you with the authentic maritime heritage of Bosa. Our expert team ensures unforgettable memories on crystal-clear Mediterranean waters.

Service cards

Fishing

Experience traditional fishing with local experts. Learn authentic fishing techniques on the beautiful Sardinian waters. Perfect for both beginners and experienced anglers.

Snorkeling

Explore vibrant marine life and crystal-clear Mediterranean waters. Discover colorful fish, coral reefs, and underwater landscapes in one of the world's most beautiful diving destinations.

Fresh Fish Dining

Enjoy delicious meals prepared on board with the freshest catch of the day. Experience authentic Sardinian cuisine while watching the sunset over the sea.

  3. 11:00

    Swim & snorkel bays
