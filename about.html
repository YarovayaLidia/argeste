About Argeste Pescaturismo

Experience authentic Sardinian fishing tourism with our family-run operation based in beautiful Bosa. For generations, we've been sharing the traditions of Mediterranean fishing, offering unforgettable journeys along the stunning coastline where crystal-clear waters meet ancient maritime heritage.

Our Mission

To preserve and share the authentic fishing traditions of Sardinia while providing sustainable, eco-friendly tourism experiences. We believe in respecting the sea, honoring our heritage, and creating lasting memories for every guest who joins us aboard.
